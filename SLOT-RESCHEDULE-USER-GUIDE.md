## â“ Common Questions

### Q: Will I lose my booking if I miss the notification?
**A:** No, your booking is automatically updated. The notification is just to inform you.

### Q: Can I reschedule again if the new time doesn't work?
**A:** Yes, contact the blood bank to request another reschedule.

### Q: What if I don't see the popup?
**A:** Check your email. If you dismissed the popup, you can view it again by logging out and back in.

### Q: How long do I have to acknowledge?
**A:** There's no deadline. The popup will continue appearing until you acknowledge it.

### Q: Can I cancel my donation?
**A:** Yes, contact the blood bank as soon as possible to cancel.

### Q: What if the email address is wrong?
**A:** Update your profile with the correct email, then contact the blood bank for a new notification.
