### Understanding Deferrals

**Temporary Deferrals** (Can donate after waiting period):
- Recent procedures (3 days - 12 months)
- Recent diseases (3 months - 2 years)
- Recent medications (3 days - 12 months)

- Heart disease
- Epilepsy
- Blood disorders
- Chronic conditions (uncontrolled diabetes, cancer, etc.)
- Organ transplant recipients
- HIV/AIDS
- Chronic Hepatitis B/C
